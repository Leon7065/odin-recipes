Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken

Description

This dish features tender chicken breasts smothered in a rich, creamy garlic Parmesan sauce, with hints of Italian herbs and a touch of white wine for depth. Served over a bed of pasta or with a side of roasted vegetables, this meal is both comforting and indulgent, perfect for a cozy evening at home.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Heavy cream
  • Chicken broth
  • Italian seasoning
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • White wine (optional)
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Season and Sear: Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Sear them in a hot skillet with olive oil until golden brown on both sides. Remove and set aside.

  2. Make the Sauce:In the same skillet, melt butter and saute minced garlic until fragrant. Add white whine (if using) and let it reduce slightly. Stir in Chicken broth and heavy cream, then simmer.

  3. Add Parmesan:Gradually whisk in grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.

  4. Combine: Return the chicken to the skillet, letting it simmer in the sauce until fully cooked and tender.

  5. Serve: Serve hot over pasta or with your choice of side, spooning extra sauce over the top. Enjoy!
