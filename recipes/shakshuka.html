Shakshuka

Description

Shakshuka is a North African and Middle Eastern meal of poached eggs in a simmering tomato sauce with spices.

Ingredients

  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • garlic
  • spices
  • poached eggs

Steps

  1. Saute the veggies.(diced onion, red bell pepper).
  2. Add garlic and spices and stir until it's nice and fragrant.
  3. Pour in 28-ounce can of whole peeled tomatoes and break them into small pieces.
  4. Once it's simmering use your spatula to make some space and than crack an egg on top.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, and cook for another 5 to 8 min until the eggs are to your liking.
