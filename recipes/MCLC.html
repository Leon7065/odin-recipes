Molten Chocolate Lava Cake

Description

This decadent dessert features a rich, chocolate cake with a delicate exterior that hides a warm, gooey center of molten chocolate. When you cut into the cake, the luscious chocolate flows out, creating a perfect contrast between the soft, slightly crisp cake and the velvety, molten core. Paired with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dusting of powdered sugar, this indulgent treat is sure to satisfy any chocolate lover’s cravings.

Ingredients

  • Semi-sweet chocolate (chopped)
  • Unsalted butter
  • Eggs
  • Egg yolks
  • Granulated sugar
  • All-purpose flour
  • Vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Melt CHocolate and Butter:In a heatproof bowl, melt the chopped chocolate and butter together, either in the microwave or over a double boiler, until smooth. Stir to combine.

  2. Mix Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla extract until well combined.

  3. Combine: Slowly add the melted chocolate mixture to the egg mixture, stirring constantly. Then fold in the flour and a pinch of salt until just combined.

  4. Fill Ramekins: Grease ramekins with butter and lightly dust with flour. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared ramekins.

  5. Bake: Bake in a preheated oven at 425°F for about 12-14 minutes, until the edges are set but the center is still soft.

  6. Serve: Let the cakes cool for a minute, then carefully invert each onto a plate. Serve immediately with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dusting of powdered sugar. Enjoy!
